🛡️ Before you ship — 30-second checklist

  • Stop using the device. Each power cycle worsens certain failures.
  • Don't try recovery software. 60% of permanent data losses are caused by DIY attempts.
  • Pack it well. Antistatic bag if possible, bubble wrap, rigid box. Avoid loose styrofoam beads (static).
  • Label the package for NEXURADATA + your case number (if already opened).
  • Don't password-protect the device before shipping — share the password separately.
  • Include a note with your name, phone, and brief description of the issue.

No antistatic bag at hand? A rigid box well-filled with bubble wrap is enough for most devices. For particularly fragile or high-value drives, prefer in-person drop-off (Option 2 or 3).
