How a case from Longueuil works

  1. You write to us. Online form, email, or call 438 813‑0592. Reply in under 24 hours.
  2. You drop off the device. By appointment at the Longueuil lab, or shipped via secure courier with a pre-paid label.
  3. Free assessment. We inspect the device in a controlled environment. No fees at this stage.
  4. Firm price and turnaround. You get a written quote with exact price, turnaround, and odds of success. Then you decide.
  5. Recovery and delivery. Data delivered on a new encrypted drive. If nothing is recovered, nothing is billed.
