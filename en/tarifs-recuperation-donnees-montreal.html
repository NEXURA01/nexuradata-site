Contractual engagement and scope

Every case begins with a free initial exchange: context, media, urgency. Once orientation is confirmed, the in-lab technical or forensic assessment is performed against a documented deposit. No billable recovery work begins without your written approval of the quote. Scope, deliverables, timelines and payment terms are set contractually.

"No data, no recovery fee" policy: on HDD/SSD/RAID recovery mandates, if recovery is not technically feasible, no recovery fee is billed (the prior assessment and any cleanroom time committed remain due). Forensic and compliance mandates are billed on deliverable, regardless of investigation outcome.
