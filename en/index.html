Why not your IT guy? Why not Geek Squad?

Three good intentions, three very different outcomes. The honest comparison, no flattery for anyone.

Option A

Your usual technician

The friend who knows a thing or two, or the local shop.

  • Cheap, sometimes free
  • Plugs the drive into Windows to "take a look" — makes physical cases worse
  • No controlled environment (dust, static)
  • No chain of custody — useless in court

Good for: reinstalling Windows. Risky for: your data.

Option B

Big-box chain (Geek Squad, etc.)

Counter in store, file shipped elsewhere.

  • Convenient, available everywhere
  • Opaque outsourcing to third-party labs
  • 3–6 week turnaround, little communication
  • Non-refundable diagnostic fees, even on failure

Good for: device warranties. Risky for: your timeline.

Frequently asked questions

What our clients ask

How much does data recovery cost?

Pricing starts at $79 for deleted files and goes up to $650+ for RAID or server cases. The initial assessment is free — you know the price before committing.

How long does recovery take?

Simple cases (deleted files, USB drives) are typically handled in 24 to 48 hours. Complex cases (RAID, damaged SSD, forensics) may take 3 to 7 business days. Emergencies are accepted at all times.

Is my data safe with you?

Absolutely. We operate under Quebec's Law 25 for personal information protection. Every case is confidential, encrypted in transit, and data is destroyed after delivery.

What happens if the recovery fails?

If no usable data can be extracted, you are not charged for the intervention. The free initial assessment helps estimate chances before we begin.

What types of media do you handle?

Hard drives (HDD), SSDs, USB keys, memory cards, phones (iPhone and Android), servers, NAS, RAID arrays, and forensic media. If your data was stored on it, we can likely recover it.

Do I need to visit in person?

No. You can describe your case by email and we arrange secure reception of your media. Case tracking is done online.
