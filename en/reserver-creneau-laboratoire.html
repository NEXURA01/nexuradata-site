What to bring

  • The device (drive, SSD, NAS, phone, memory card).
  • If possible: cables, power supply, original enclosure.
  • Photo ID (for chain of custody).
  • Password or PIN if applicable (never sent by email).

What happens at drop-off

  1. Welcome and signing of the chain-of-custody form.
  2. Photo of the physical condition of the device.
  3. Case created in our system (6-character reference).
  4. Assessment within 24 to 72 hours depending on device type.
  5. Written quote with firm price and turnaround. You decide next.

No fees at booking. If nothing is recovered, nothing is billed.
